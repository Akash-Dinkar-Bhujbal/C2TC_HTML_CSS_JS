As a social activity every year we are organizing blood donation camp in the college campus. This year on 20th February 2020, CSI, MESA, PESA, NSS and ITSA student associations have organized the blood donation camp in the campus with Wadia Hospital , Mumbai. There was good response from the students and staff members of the institute. Total 75 donors donated blood on the occasion. The local members Mr. Rajabhai Kothari and Mr. Satish Pimpare supported the students for organizing the camp. Dr. Prasad Patil, Dr. Honey of Wadia Hospital appreciated the students with certificate for their efforts during the camp.
